About

Physical Therapy Reimagined

Academic Background

Dr. Belle Lau PT, DPT has been practicing physical therapy for 6 months and earned her Doctorate degree from the University of St. Augustine in San Marcos, California with High Honors at the end of 2023. She studied Integrative Biology for her undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley, completing and earning her Bachelor’s degree in 2020.

How it Started

Belle’s love for physical therapy began in high school where she suffered multiple injuries while on the varsity cross country team. Belle’s high school senior project focused on instructing middle and high school athletes on injury prevention techniques through countless hours of research and shadowing licensed physical therapists. After undergraduate, Belle worked as the lead aide at a private outpatient orthopedic clinic, helping patients with their exercise treatment plans and applying various modalities such as electrical stimulation. In PT school, Belle practiced as a student physical therapist in post-op rehabilitation, worker’s comp, and skilled nursing settings.

What Sets Us Apart

Belle’s main passion is sports medicine physical therapy and hopes to make a difference in athletes and active individuals, both young and old, not only by treating the injury, but preventing it before it even happens. Revolutionary Physical Therapy aims to create client satisfaction through improved physical performance. We offer private 1-on-1 therapy sessions, injury prevention programs, bi-annual physical therapy checkups, and tiered memberships.
